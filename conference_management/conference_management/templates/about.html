About Us

This conference management system is a software platform designed to streamline and automate the organization, planning, and execution of conferences and management of research papers. It typically encompasses various features and functionalities to assist the users.
Key features include:

  1. Event Planning and Scheduling: Enables organizers to plan event schedules, sessions, and activities, and manage venues, speakers, and resources.
  2. Registration and Ticketing: Facilitates user registration, ticketing, and processing of their submitted papers,also offering various ticket types and discounts.
  3. Abstract Submission and Review: Allows authors to submit abstracts, papers, or proposals online, and provides a system for peer review and selection of presentations.
  4. Communication and Collaboration: Provides tools for communication between authors, users, sponsors, and chairs, such as email notifications, messaging, and discussion forums.
  5. Attendee Management: Manages author , chair and other users' profiles.
  6. Paper Management: Tracks uploading,reviewing, acceptance and rejection of papers.Also repository for earlier papers.
  7. On-site Management: Offers log-in and log-out features for both chairs and authors.
  8. Analytics and Reporting: Provides insights into attendee demographics, paper popularity, feedback, and other key performance metrics for evaluation and improvement.
Overall, this conference paper management system helps streamline the complexities of organizing and managing papers, enhancing the experience for both chairs and authors.
